

<p>magix illuminate a web application framework for developers.&nbsp;&nbsp;
built on top of a design pattern called; active events.&nbsp;&nbsp;this off-loads a lot
of your burdons.&nbsp;&nbsp;your code becomes agile, and easily replaceable and extendible.&nbsp;&nbsp;
your finished applications also become easily scalable, and possible to 
partition as you see fit, through the means of divide and conquer, on your
application server park</p>

<p>magix is built in c# on mono</p>
